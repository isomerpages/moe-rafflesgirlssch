<p>Celebrating 145 Years of Nurturing Daughters of a Better Age!</p>
<p>The indomitable Rafflesian Spirit filled the air as we joyfully celebrated
our 145th Founder’s Day! Embracing this year’s theme, One Heart, One Voice,
the Rafflesian community came together in unity to honour our rich heritage
and shared values. We were delighted to have marine ecologist Dr Siti Maryam
Yaakub (Class of 1997), Senior Director of the International Blue Carbon
Institute at Conservation International, as our esteemed Guest of Honour.</p>
