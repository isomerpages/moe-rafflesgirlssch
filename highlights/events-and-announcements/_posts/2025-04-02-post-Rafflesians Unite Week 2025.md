<p>RU Week 2025 was a vibrant celebration of school spirit, uniting the RGS
community through a series of engaging activities organised by the RU Week
OT. Designed to foster a sense of belonging and pride, the week featured
diverse programmes that showcased the energy and enthusiasm of Rafflesians.</p>
<p>Students participated in a variety of interactive booths, including face
painting, a photo booth, and the Raffles Runfair, where SVIA and CmPS teams
set up booths to raise awareness for student-initiated social causes, encouraging
meaningful engagement with the community.</p>

<p>During Dining with the Track Stars, students sat down to enjoy performances
by their fellow schoolmates while cheering and showing their support.</p>
<p>Students dressed in green, black, and white for GBW Day, showing their
collective spirit. The festivities culminated in House Day, with students
proudly dressed in their House colours, celebrating camaraderie and unity.</p>
<p>RU Week 2025 was a testament to the strong bonds within the RGS community,
reinforcing the joy and pride of being a Rafflesian.</p>
