<p>Our students were recently featured in <em>Tamil Murasu</em> for their participation
in the Singapore Tamil Writers’ Association’s 140th Story Forum held at
the National Library.</p>
<p>Deekshitha Senthil, Dharshitha Selvaraju, and Gayathri Suresh Harisukarita
presented their reflections on <em>Oru Thuli Santhosham</em> (A Drop of Happiness)
by Chitra Ramesh, contributing thoughtfully to the literary discussion
alongside members of the Tamil literary community.</p>
<p>We are proud of our students for engaging deeply with literature and for
representing the school with confidence and insight.</p>
