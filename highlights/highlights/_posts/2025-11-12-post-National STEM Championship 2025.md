<p>From March to May 2025, a team of four Year 3 students represented RGS
at the National STEM Championship (NSTEMC), a national-level competition
designed to nurture scientific inquiry, inventive thinking, and creative
problem-solving among youths across Singapore.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Cai Yuhan (S309)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Liu Xinmeng (S310)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Zhang Jinghan (S309)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Li Pengfei (S312)</p>
<p></p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>They advanced to the Quarter Finals and also earned a place in the Wild
Card Round.</p>
<p>The NSTEMC aims to inspire future generations of scientists and engineers
through challenges that integrate Science, Technology, Engineering and
Mathematics. The team engaged fully in this spirit, reflecting the strong
interdisciplinary culture and spirit of inquiry in RGS.</p>
