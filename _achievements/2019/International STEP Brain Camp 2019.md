## International STEP Brain Camp 2019

<img src="/images/step1.jpg" style="width:85%">

_RGS Year 4 students (from left) Cao Yuqing, Wu Jingya, Tanya Tan Xin Yu and Lucia Li, together with Teacher-Mentor Mrs Diana Ng (centre), participated in the International STEP Brain Camp 2019._

Four Year 4 RGS students represented Singapore at the International STEP Brain Camp 2019, where they took part in a series of hands-on activities and scientific lectures. They also worked with the team from Philippines for a presentation on a neuroscience research topic.

The International STEP Brain Camp 2019 was held from 9-15 June. The purpose of the camp is to inspire and educate secondary and tertiary students on brain research. In addition, it also fosters interaction and friendship between students from ASEAN countries.
