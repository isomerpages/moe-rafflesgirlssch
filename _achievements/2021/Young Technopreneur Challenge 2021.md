## Young Technopreneur Challenge 2021

Jointly organised by [Junior Achievement Singapore](https://www.ja.org.sg/), HP Inc. and South West Community Development Council (CDC), the Young Technopreneur Challenge 2021 aims to support students to take ownership of the world they live in and actively develop solutions to address environmental concerns.

<img src="/images/young1.png" style="width:90%">
<figcaption>  A total of 171 students from 19 participating schools stretched their imagination to think global, innovate, create and pitch their ideas & concepts by making use of STEM and technology.
 </figcaption>

<figure>

<figcaption>  Congratulations to Aislinn Goh, Charlene Mak, Gan Kah Shuen & Kenzie Li, from RGS team ‘Codex’ for their proposed product the ‘HandiShelf’, a rotatable drawer system that would bring the desired item to an accessible range, all at a push of a button. They won ‘Best Presentation’ and also emerged as the Champion for the competition.
