## House Banner Reveal 2022

<img src="/images/banner.jpg" style="width:85%">

Each year, our five Houses capture their aspirations in their banner designs, which feature their House themes for the year, House mascots and values. Our 2022 House banners were unveiled on 8 February via a virtual curtain reveal ceremony. Watch the video below for a closer look at the banners and find out more about what each House has to say about their themes for the year.
