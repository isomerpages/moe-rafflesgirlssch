<p>Enter the World of Whimsy this Aesthetics Day!</p>
<p>Rising to the challenge of bringing creativity and imagination to life,
Aesthetics Day invited students into a whimsical wonderland to explore
new ideas, embrace possibilities, and to think beyond convention.</p>
<p>From crochet and graffiti to stamping, jewellery making, cosplay design,
dance, music, and science crafts, students expressed themselves in vibrant
ways.</p>
<p>Thank you to all workshop facilitators, staff, and students for making
it such a delightfully whimsical day!</p>
