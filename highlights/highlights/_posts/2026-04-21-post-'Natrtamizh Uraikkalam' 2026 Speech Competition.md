<p>A proud moment for our students Panchakshari (301), Varshikaa and Nithya
Sree (303), who emerged 1st place winners at the 'Natrtamizh Uraikkalam'
speech competition, held at the Umar Pulavar Tamil Language Centre, with
their achievement also featured in Tamil Murasu. Centred on the theme "Artificial
Intelligence and Tamil Language", the competition brought together 90 students
from 30 schools, where participants explored Al's role in language and
the importance of its responsible use through creative presentations and
speeches.</p>
<p>Reflecting on her experience, Varshikaa (303) shared: "Participating in
'Natramizh Uraikkalam' 2026 and clinching the challenge trophy was a meaningful
experience for our team. Despite challenges such as schedule clashes and
limited preparation time, we stayed committed, adapted quickly, and supported
one another. This journey strengthened our resilience and teamwork. We
are deeply grateful to our teacher, classmates, and families for their
unwavering support."</p>
