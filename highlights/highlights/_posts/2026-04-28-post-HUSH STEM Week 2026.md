<p>HUSH-STEM Week 2026 kicked off with an exciting lineup of keynote speakers,
workshops, and dialogue sessions, transforming the school into a vibrant
space for curiosity, creativity, and discovery From inspiring keynote speakers
such as Stick 'Em, Wateroam, Dr Calvin Wong, and EDP Renewables APAC, students
explored how innovation, technology, and purpose can come together to shape
the future. 💡🌎🔬</p>
<p>Beyond the stage, hands-on workshops invited students to experience HuSH-STEM
in action through activities such as water filtration, sustainability entrepreneurship,
web development, financial literacy, and upcycling.</p>
<p>These experiences were deepened through dialogue sessions with industry
professionals such as Google, HSA, SIT, SMU, and NTU, where students explored
human-centred technology, cutting-edge medicine, and real-world innovation
while discovering how these fields can shape meaningful careers and impact.</p>
