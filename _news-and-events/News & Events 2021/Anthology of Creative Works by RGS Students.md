## Anthology of Creative Works by RGS Students

<p><a href="https://inretrospect2020.weebly.com/">
<img style="width:100%" src="/images/inretrospect.png">
</a></p>

_[in.re.trospect](https://inretrospect2020.weebly.com/)_ is an anthology of creative works written by RGS Year 1-4 students from 2020. Put together by a group of Year 4 students from the class of 2020, _[in.re.trospect](https://inretrospect2020.weebly.com/)_ sought to capture the reflection of students on diverse issues through works of prose, poetry, photography and art. Categorised under the three broad themes of Retrospect, Introspect and Prospect, visitors of the site can look forward to original works conveying the unique perspectives and voices of RGS students.  
  
Click [here](https://inretrospect2020.weebly.com/) to explore the creative works.
