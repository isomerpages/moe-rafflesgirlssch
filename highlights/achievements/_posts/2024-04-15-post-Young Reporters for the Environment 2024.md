<p>Our Y3 ASEAN Scholar, Narudee Teplertboon, recently earned second place
in the Young Reporters for the Environment writing competition with her
compelling article on climate change. Titled ‘Saving The Earth’s Biggest
Brains,’ the piece delves into the plight of sperm whales, highlighting
their vulnerability to threats induced by climate change.</p>
<p>The YRE programme empowers young people&nbsp;to take an educated stand
on environmental issues they feel strongly about and gives them a platform
to articulate these issues through the media of writing, photography or
video.&nbsp;</p>
