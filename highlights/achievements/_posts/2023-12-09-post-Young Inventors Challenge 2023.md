<p>Organised by the Association of Science, Technology, and Innovation (ASTI),
the competition aims to develop future inventors who will be the positive
change-makers of tomorrow by encouraging students to find innovative solutions
to current-day problems. For 2023, the finalists were required to invent
a product and/or process capable of providing a resolution to a certain
level and having an impact on this year’s theme: ‘Sustainable Development
Goals (SDGs)’.</p>
<p>A total of 217 participating teams from 8 different countries (Brunei,
India, Indonesia, Malaysia, the Philippines, Singapore, Sri Lanka, and
Thailand) were selected for the finals of the competition, and only 30
teams were further shortlisted for the online viva session. RGS is honored
to be the <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=o5rm_qmdnOM" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">only two teams</a> from
Singapore shortlisted.</p>
