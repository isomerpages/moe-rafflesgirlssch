<p>A warm welcome and our sincere thanks to the Dutch Ambassador in Singapore,
Anneke Adema for her visit to RGS, where she graciously presented a beautiful
gift of tulips — a symbol deeply rooted in Dutch culture. 🌷</p>
<p>Ambassador Adema engaged our students in a meaningful cultural exchange,
sharing her insights and offering thoughtful advice, while students had
the opportunity to ask questions and gain a deeper understanding of her
experiences and perspectives.</p>
<p>We are truly honoured to have hosted Ambassador Adema and are grateful
for this enriching and memorable visit.</p>
