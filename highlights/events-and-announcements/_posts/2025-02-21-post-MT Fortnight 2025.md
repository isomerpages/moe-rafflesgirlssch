<p>Our students embarked on a meaningful "Journey with Languages and Appreciation
of Culture" during Mother Tongue Fortnight 2025!</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">

beauty of their Mother Tongue Languages (MTL) while deepening their connection
to diverse cultures.</p>
