<p>We hope RU Week brought everyone a little magic and a trip down memory
lane. Kicking off with our Raffles Winx theme, we celebrated the sparkle
and spirit of our girls while building on the school’s 2026 theme, “Deep
Roots, Strong Wings”, empowering students to find their wings and grow
with confidence.</p>

<p>With a wide array of activities curated by PB External Welfare and our
student groups, the week was filled with excitement. The hall assembly
set the tone with a performance on discovering one’s wings, while “Winxstop”
featured ensemble renditions of popular tunes like Howl’s Moving Castle
and Ponyo, alongside vibrant dance and vocal acts. Paired with such entertainment
were also free snacks and karaoke segments for anyone who dropped by “Winxstop”!</p>
<p>Lined up were also the Raffles Runfair booths that showcased students’
passion projects, from balloon sculpting and cubing to advocacy efforts
on sustainability, animal welfare, ethical consumerism, migrant worker
stories and inclusivity.</p>
<p>For an extra sprinkle of fairy dust, our first-ever “Special Days” brought
retro fun to life, with crowd favourites like themed photobooths and a
typewriter station for heartfelt notes.</p>
