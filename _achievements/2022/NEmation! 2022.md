## N.E.mation! 2022

<img src="/images/20220320_120433.jpg" style="width:85%">

Congratulations to Team 3s.f., comprising RGS Year 4 students (from left) Neo Joe Ern, Hilary Chee Xin Yi and Sivamuthu Nihidha Varshni for winning Special Mention at N.E.mation! 2022!

Held annually, N.E.mation! is a national digital animation and storyboard competition for students and youths to express their thoughts about Total Defence.
