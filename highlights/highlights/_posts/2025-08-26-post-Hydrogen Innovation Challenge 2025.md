<p>Our students took part in the Hydrogen Innovation Challenge 2025, organised
by the NUS Centre for Hydrogen Innovations. This national-level competition
encourages students to explore innovative solutions related to hydrogen
and sustainable energy.</p>
<p>In Round 1, teams collaborated to complete a quiz on green energy. The
top 15 teams advanced to Round 2, where they designed an infographic on
the theme “Singapore’s Green Energy Pathway” and presented it through a
live performance.</p>
<p>We are proud that three RGS teams advanced to Round 2 and achieved commendable
results in this competition.</p>
