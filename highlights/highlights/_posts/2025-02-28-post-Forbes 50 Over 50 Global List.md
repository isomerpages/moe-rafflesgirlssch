<p>We are pleased to share that Professor Jackie Ying (Class of 1982), our
current RGS Board Member, has been featured in the inaugural Forbes 50
Over 50 Global list, recognising women trailblazers making a worldwide
impact.</p>
<p>A pioneering scientist in nanoscience and nanotechnology, Professor Ying
has contributed over 400 research papers and 200 patents, significantly
advancing the field. Her groundbreaking work continues to inspire future
generations of scientists and innovators.</p>
<p>Congratulations to Professor Ying on this well-deserved recognition!</p>
